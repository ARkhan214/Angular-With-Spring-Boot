Who We Are

Pioneer of SME (Small and Medium Enterprise) Banking in Bangladesh.

Founded in 2001 by Sir Fazle Hasan Abed KCMG, who also established BRAC - the world's largest NGO.

The 4 cornerstones our business model are: Governance, Compliance, Ethics and Transparency.

Offers comprehensive range of banking solutions, including state-of-the-art digital solutions, to its 1.1 million customers.

Unmatched value-chain through branch network, ATMs & CDMs, country's highest number of SME unit offices and Agent Banking outlets.

Commendable financial results, with the best credit rating in Bangladesh from some of the best local and global agencies.

Founding member and the only representative from Bangladesh to the Global Alliance for Banking on Values (GABV); signatory to the UN Global Compact and a member of the Financial Alliance for Women.

With a mission help people prosper, BRAC Bank aspires to be the best bank in Bangladesh.
